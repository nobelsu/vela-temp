*   **Institution Ranking:** All institutions are ranked "200+". While not low, it's not indicative of a top-tier educational background often associated with "outlier success" in highly competitive VC-backed startups.
*   **Lack of direct F&B education:** There's no specific education in hospitality, culinary arts, retail management, or food science.
